My First Humanised Article

Goal: Turn AI-sounding text into natural, human writing (without changing meaning).

Example

AI-style:

In today’s rapidly evolving digital landscape, individuals are increasingly leveraging artificial intelligence to enhance productivity and improve efficiency.

Humanised:

These days, a lot of people use AI tools to get work done faster and make their writing clearer.

My simple rewrite rules

  • Use shorter sentences.
  • Replace “formal filler phrases” with simple words.
  • Add a real-world example if needed.
  • Read it out loud—if it sounds robotic, rewrite again.
